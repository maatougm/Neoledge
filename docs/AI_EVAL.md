## Methodology

Each fixture seeds a temporary project on the live backend, drives the questionnaire + transcripts through the existing admin/PM APIs, then calls `/cahier-des-charges/preview` to obtain the AI output. The output is scored on three axes and combined as a weighted sum:

- **50%** Fact-grounding — `mustMention` strings present (case-insensitive).
- **30%** Anti-hallucination — `mustNotMention` strings absent.
- **20%** French style — judged 1-10 by `glm-4.5-air` (different from the model under test to avoid self-flattery), scaled to /100.

The final per-fixture score is **capped** by the section-completeness percentage — a cahier missing required top-level keys is treated as unusable regardless of textual coverage.
